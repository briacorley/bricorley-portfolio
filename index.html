About Me

Hello! My name is Bri Corley and I am a junior journalism major at the University of Maryland. My interests in journalism include media law, investigative reporting, editing, writing, and more. I was born and raised in the metro D.C.

I have worked nearly every position in a newsroom. I've edited, written, proposed ideas, helped publish, anything you can think of. I think I thrive in environments with a focus on crime, courts, and feature writing. I also believe I am skilled in interview technique.

Outside of journalism, I am passionate about animal rescue, sexual education, and volunteer work. I volunteer at clinics, animal shelters, and women's refuge centers in my free time. I am an active member of the Gamma Theta Chapter of Alpha Chi Omega sorority.
